=======================
Forever Diamond
=======================


Make & Model 
====================

Forever Diamond is a self made machine.

BOM: 

   * AsRock TRX40 Creator mainboard
   * AMD Ryzen Threadripper 3970x
   * Noctua NH U14s + NFA15
   * Corsair Vengeance DDR4 4000MHz
   * Be Quiet Pure Base 500DX + 6 Noctua industrial fans
   * Corsair AX1500i


Intended use
====================

Forever diamond is intended for molecular dynamics runs.
